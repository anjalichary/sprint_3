A collection of the work that I have done for Dreams For Schools which includes Graphic Design, Website Design, and Social Media.

01 What is Dreams for Schools?

Dreams for Schools is a 501(c)(3) that oversees programs and initiatives that contribute to educating youth about Science, Technology, Engineering & Math fields. The main outreach program of the organization is the AppJam+ initiative.

02 Program Graphics

Dreams for Schools offers a multitude of programs, some of which include: Engineering for Kids, Coding Games with Scratch, Website Development, and AppJamPlus. I was tasked with redesigning the graphics that were assigned to each program.

03 Program Marketing (Fliers & Social Media)

A compilation of program marketing materials used to get parents to sign their kids up for the summer & fall seasons.

04 General Social Media

A compilation of social media graphics for the company's Instagram.

05 CAKE Kit

CAKE stands for Cool Activities for Kids to Engineer.

The kit contains easy-to-follow instructions through an educational context that allows for an enjoyable learning experience. The kit provides each student with all of the materials necessary to complete each project.

I was tasked with designing and laying out the booklet.
